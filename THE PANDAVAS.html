ABOUT THE PANDAVAS COMPANY PVT. LTD.

The Pandavas Company Pvt. Ltd. is inspired by the legendary heroes of the Indian epic Mahabharata. We are committed to promoting values such as bravery, righteousness, and unwavering commitment to dharma (righteousness).

OUR MOTIVE

Our motive is to inspire and guide people towards living a life of integrity, courage, and honor, just like The Pandavas.

RULES

  • Always act with honesty and integrity.
  • Stand up for what is right, even in the face of adversity.
  • Respect and support your family and friends.
  • Strive for excellence in all your endeavors.

MEMBERS

  • Yudhishthira
  • Bheema
  • Arjuna
  • Nakula
  • Sahadeva
  • Karna

CONCLUSION

The Pandavas Company Pvt. Ltd. embodies virtue and courage. We remind our community that no matter the challenges we face, staying true to our values and supporting each other is what truly makes us strong.

OUR BUSINESS PLANS

CAFE

Our cafe offers a variety of light meals and drinks perfect for a quick bite or a relaxing day out.

  • Coffee: Espresso, Cappuccino, Latte, Americano
  • Tea: Green Tea, Black Tea, Herbal Tea
  • Snacks: Croissants, Muffins, Cookies, Brownies
  • Sandwiches: Grilled Chicken, Veggie Delight, Tuna
  • Pastries: Scones, Danish, Cheesecake

BAR

Relax and unwind with a drink at our bar, serving a wide range of cocktails, mocktails, and spirits.

  • Cocktails: Margarita, Mojito, Martini, Cosmopolitan
  • Mocktails: Virgin Mojito, Fruit Punch, Lemonade
  • Beer & Spirits: Craft Beers, Whiskey, Vodka, Rum
  • Appetizers: Nachos, Fries, Chicken Wings

RESTAURANT

Our restaurant offers a full dining experience, serving delicious meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

  • Starters: Soup of the Day, Garlic Bread, Spring Rolls
  • Main Courses: Butter Chicken, Veg Tikka Masala, Grilled Salmon
  • Vegetarian: Paneer Tikka, Veg Biryani, Stuffed Bell Peppers
  • Salads: Caesar Salad, Greek Salad, Quinoa Salad
  • Desserts: Chocolate Lava Cake, Gulab Jamun, Ice Cream
